# Week 12 — Challenges

Two open-ended problems with no single right answer. The exercises drilled the mechanics; the challenges test judgment — the thing that separates someone who *runs* the tuning loop from someone who *decides* what a database should be.

1. **[Challenge 1 — Design a production schema](challenge-01-design-a-production-schema.md)** — turn a product description into a schema that meets a latency budget by construction, not by later tuning.
2. **[Challenge 2 — Defend a tuning decision](challenge-02-defend-a-tuning-decision.md)** — a skeptical reviewer pushes back on a change you made. Defend it with evidence, or concede with grace.

These are optional but strongly recommended — they are the closest thing in the course to a real design review and a real code review. Do them with a peer if you can; arguing a schema out loud is where the learning is.
